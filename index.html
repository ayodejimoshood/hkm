Introduction

HKM Professional Services (Chartered Accountants) was established in February 2008 to provide a wide range of professional services within the Nigerian economy. Our offerings are organised into three major categories:

Audit services

  • Statutory audit
  • Internal audit
  • Forensics, investigation & compliance

Business services

  • Financial accounting
  • Due diligence review
  • Business advisory, fixed assets verification
  • Independent controls & process review

Consulting

  • Accounting & MIS
  • Feasibility studies & specialised training
  • Tax management & consultancy
  • IFRS conversion, investment & executive selection

Core services

Depth across assurance, operations, and advisory.

Statutory audit

Assurance on the truth and fairness of financial information, internal operations, and statutory compliance. Our methodology aligns with International Auditing Standards, CAMA, and applicable national standards.

A controls-based approach assesses reporting practices, business risk, and internal controls—giving comfort that statements reflect true performance.

Internal audit

We help strengthen internal audit through:

  • Review of effectiveness and enhanced methodologies
  • Transformation from transactional bottleneck to strategic enabler
  • COSO framework adoption and quality assurance
  • Full or partial outsourcing and tooling support
  • Tailored training for internal auditors

Financial accounting

Write-up and maintenance of accounting records, processing transactions, and regular management reporting. We maintain receivables, payables, fixed assets, inventory, and the general ledger for SMEs.

Staff can be seconded to stabilise weak finance teams. We implement and manage systems from payroll through SAGE, Peachtree, and Tally to enterprise platforms such as Oracle and SAP.

Due diligence

Structured verification of facts around transactions, including:

  • Legal
  • Financial
  • Management
  • Marketing
  • Operations & technical

Independent controls & process review

  • Evaluation of management controls
  • Assurance on business technology management
  • Risk management solutions
  • Traditional third-party assurance

Reassurance that systems, processes, and risk procedures operate effectively.

Accounting & MIS

System design for growing organisations; reviews for larger entities scaling activity and growth.

  • Uniform coding and sequential transaction recording
  • Internal controls for transparency and accountability
  • Standard records and documentation
  • Timely management information
  • Payroll design, computerisation, and networking
  • Induction for accounts personnel

Feasibility studies

Multidisciplinary support for lenders and investors assessing project viability:

  • Market
  • Managerial
  • Technical
  • Financial

Specialised training

Open and in-company programmes in finance, accounting, and soft skills—tailored to your organisation.

Examples include finance for non-finance managers, investment appraisal, presentation and communication skills, project management, and stress management.

Tax management & consultancy

Company income tax

Planning, computation, exemptions and incentives, filings, and resolution of queries through agreed returns.

Personal income tax

Employee matters with State Internal Revenue Services for Nigerian and expatriate staff.

Petroleum profit tax

Support for petroleum-related activities and industry tax incentives.

VAT

Input planning, output reconciliation, monthly returns, and audit resolution.

Withholding tax

Guidance on rates and returns, audits, reconciliation, credit notes, and claims with revenue authorities.

Capital market tax

Compliance under CITA for primary and secondary market operators; liaison with FIRS tax desks on WHT and VAT.

We also assist with tax clearance and acceptance certificates, tax audits, new-project tax implications, and other specialised tax needs.

Our fees

Fees reflect anticipated workload, staff level, and time to complete. Timing of payments is agreed with management before fieldwork begins.

Clients also reimburse assignment-related expenses, including local transport, travel and accommodation, printing, and communication.
